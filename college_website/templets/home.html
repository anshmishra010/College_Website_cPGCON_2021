About cPGCON 2021

cPGCON 2021

cPGCON 2021 is a Post Graduate Conference, an annual event for PG students and research scholars, organized by the Department of Computer Engineering, Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering, Pune in association with Board of Studies in Computer Engineering, Savitribai Phule Pune University. cPGCON2021 will provide a platform to discuss latest innovations, share experiences and learn new developments in the various domains. It will work as a forerunner and facilitator for PG students theoretical and practical studies and research scholars will pose their research work on the frontiers of knowledge and skills in the area.

About The Institute

Prof. M. N. Navale has established Sinhgad Technical Education Society (STES) in the year 1993 with the aim of imparting quality education in the field of Engineering, Management, Computer, Pharmacy, Medical Education, Physiotherapy, Nursing, Architecture, Hotel Management and basic school education. At present, Sinhgad Technical Education Society spreads over 13 Campuses, having more than 111 Institutes. Nearly 70,000 students are taking quality education under the guidance of well qualified academic staff

STES's Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering (SKNCOE) has become synonymous with quality Engineering Education. SKNCOE was established in the year 2001 offering degree courses in Computer, E & TC, IT, Mechanical. All the above courses are approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. SKNCOE has Research Center to pursue Ph.D. in Computer Engineering, along with post graduation courses in Computer Engineering. The Institute is Accredited by NAAC

About The Department

The Computer Engineering Department started in the year 2001, with an intake of 60 which has increased to current intake of 300. The Department has well qualified and experience faculty and well equipped laboratories, Classrooms and Library for imparting the course contents and the knowledge beyond PG and Ph.D. in Computer Engineering. Currently SKNCOE research center has 58 Research Scholar are pursing research.
