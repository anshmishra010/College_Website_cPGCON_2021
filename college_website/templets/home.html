About cPGCON 2021

cPGCON 2021

cPGCON-2021 is a Post Graduate Conference, an annual event for PG students and research scholars, organized by the Department of Computer Engineering, Smt.Kasibai Navle College Of Engineering, Vadagaon, in association with Board of Studies in Computer Engineering, Savitribai Phule Pune University. This conference will ide a platform to discuss latest innovations, share experience, and learn new developments in the area. This conference will work as a forerunner and facilitator for the analytical and practical research. PG students and research scholars present their research work in the frontiers of domain expertise and innovation. The important objective of this conference to develop association with world leading knowledge centres and expertise

About The Institute

Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering was established in 2001 with 3 branches and 180 sanctioned intake. The institute has progressed geometrically in all dimensions e.g. scale, quality of education and brand in the field.

SKNCOE is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune.Departments of SKNCOE are accredited by theNational Board of Accreditation / NAAC.Team of SKNCOE students has been continuously participating and winning in the National Level events like BAJA, SUPRA,EBAJA and ROBOCON since last couple of years.SKNCOE has an exclusive E-Cell to promote entrepreneur skills, 600+ research publications in last four years.

About The Department

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in the year 2001 with the affiliation to University of Pune (now SPPU) and approved by AICTE with objective of divulging quality education in the field of Computing.

The future belongs to those who are Knowledgeable, Thinkers & Principled. Guided by these virtues, we run our programs with Undergraduate with the intake of 60 which eventually has amplied to current intake of 300, Postgraduate (Computer Engineering) with intake of 18 and Ph.D program in Computer Engineering 41 Ph.D. scholars pursuing research at SKN COE (Computer) Research Center.

We have our recent graduates & staff studying at London School of Economics, Stanford University, Carnegie Mellol University,New York University, Michigan University, IIT, IMT, MDI,AAU and our faculty are pursuing their doctoral studies at reputed European universities. Department of Computer Engineering has Entrepreneurship Cell through which students get opportunity for Internship with reputed companies. The department is committed to foster an effective teaching/learning environment, personal attention for academic excellence and promotion for Co-curricular and Extra-curricular activities for students.
